Universe Overview

Inside the world of
Jujutsu Kaisen

In Jujutsu Kaisen, curses are born from human negativity and jujutsu sorcerers are trained to neutralize them. This showcase focuses on the key fighters, mentors, and antagonists whose choices shape the major arcs of the series.
